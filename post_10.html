Setting Up Light Scenese with Samsung SmartThings

Posted by Andrew Huebert on Apr 17, 2020

Recently my father and I have been adding IoT devices and other implementations of the Samsung SmartThings application. Our most recent project has been getting the right light switches that dim and can be controlled by Wi-Fi signals.

We finally found the correct light switches a few weeks ago. We were distracted by the "Notification Light" that came with the switches. But we then pivoted to setting up whole scenes and "Moods" for the lights.

Firstly, we had to set up a "Movie" scene that would dim everything and turn on the TV. We set it up so that when we say "It's Movie Time" the Kitchen and Living Room lights dim, the color LED's behind the TV turn on to Blue, and the TV turns on the TV with the Surround Sound.

Lastly, my father wanted to add a "Spring" mood for the kitchen. During the rainier times my dad decided to make a "Blue Sky" theme. So when asked for a "Spring" mood, the kitchen lights switch to varying shades of blue and lighter colors. This was as far as we got with our scenes, we are thinking of adding more that start music, or open the garage in the morning.
