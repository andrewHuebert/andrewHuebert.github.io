Connecting a NVIDIA Shield to a SageTV Client

Posted by Andrew Huebert on May 8, 2020

I recently obtained a NVIDIA Shield for the TV in my room. This Shield is similar to an Amazon Fire Stick, where it plugs into the HDMI port of the TV and connects to the internet. Once connected it has all sorts of apps, and access to the Google Play Store to obtain other apps a user might want.

To connect the Shield to the SageTV that I have set up in the living room there are a couple of prerequisites needed. Firstly, we need to be connected to the same network, and secondly we must obtain the SageTV Mini Client app from the Google Play Store.

Once the Shield is on the proper network and the app is downloaded it is quite siple to connect. First we open the application on the TV and "Browse" the netowrk for the SageTV I have set up in the living room. I select the proper client and the app automatically connects to the client in the living room.

The app connects to the client and opens up a page that looks similar to the Home Page of the client in the living room. We then go into Settings and select "Use Mini Client" to ensure that the system works as it does in the living room. And lastly, we set up the application to automatically connect to this client when opened. In the Application Settings you can set it to connect to a client, and if it fails it will simply return to the home page. And now the NVIDIA Shield is connected.
